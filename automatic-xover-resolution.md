# Summary

Instead interactive resolution of ionic cross over method, the feature should resolve it automatically, and pop up a window explain the process for better understanding once the user choose the two elements. 

Another issue, user should be able to replace the element with another element any time and restart the process. Means clear the selected elements in drop zone and final compound, back to the initial state.

Upon a selection of an element, once the compatible elements are identified, in addition to highlight them, please show popup window what type of bonding that can be formed between them, whether it is `ionic`, `covalent`, or `metallic`. In the selection tray, highlight the compatible elements with opacity or gray, incompatible with opacity 0.4
For those compatible, There are 3 possible bonding types. Determine which one is the most stable bonding is beyond the scope of this app. So, just show the 3 bonding types. The user should choose one of them. 

* `for ionic bonding`, based on the valence electron of the two elements,the cross over method will be applied automatically. The ion and cation charge will be determined based on the valence electron of the two elements. So, the ion charge is always positive for metal and negative for non metal. Same apply to polyatomic ions.

* `for metallic bonding`,  based on the valence electron of each element, the number of valence electrons to share will be displayed. 

* `for covalent bonding`, based on the valence electron of each element, the number of valence electrons to share will be displayed.

* Automatic resolution of ionic cross over method
